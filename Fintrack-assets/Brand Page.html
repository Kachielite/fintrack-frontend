Rationale

Why this mark.

Vela means sail. A sail catches wind and converts it into forward motion — a useful metaphor for a personal finance app. The brief was clear: no coins, no charts, no shields, no dollar signs. So the mark earns its place by being a single, confident geometric form.

01
The sail catches wind
The right-bulging silhouette is a sail with the wind in it. Asymmetry is not a bug — it's the whole point. Static sails don't go anywhere.
02
The amber rises
The amber sliver runs diagonally up-and-right. It reads as the mast of the sail, and — if you look twice — as a trendline going up. Negative space doing double duty.
03
Confident, not loud
No gradients, no shines, no badges. Olive ground says "premium" without screaming it. The mark sits next to Linear and Wise on a home screen — not next to a casino app.
